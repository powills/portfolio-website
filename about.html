About Me

My Story

Welcome to my website! My name is Paige Wills, and I am a Junior UI/UX Designer. I recently completed my Certification in UI/UX design from CareerFoundry and hold a BFA in Studio Arts from Concordia University.

My many artistic ventures in Graphic Novels, Illustration, Ceramics, Graphic Design and more lead me to discover my true passion which was becoming a UI Designer. I have never been more fulfilled in my field of interest.

As a person I am passionate, driven, organized and hardworking. I place a lot of pride in the work that I do, and that can sometimes hinder my final execution of the product because I expect perfection from myself. This is something that I continue to work on. I am proficient in using Adobe XD, InVision, and I am working on becoming more knowledgeable using Sketch, Figma and Photoshop. The skills that I have acquired are in typography, iconography, color theory, user interviews, testing and research, wireframing and prototyping.

My hobbies include designing, drawing (both digitally and traditionally), watercolor painting, playing guitar, watching movies and coffee.
